Second Principle of Stratagema | Explain

Strategy is an environment for thinking, not for action. It is where we explore the relationship between the physical and the metaphysical dimensions of reality. At its core, strategy is a theory of how the world works.

The Strategus seeks a higher level of understanding by deploying tactical systems, each designed as a unique operational environment to test that theory. This process is what unlocks the potential for true change across four dimensions:

  1. To change my own way of thinking.
  2. To change my understanding of the world.
  3. To change my organization.
  4. And only then, to change the world by intervening in a way that achieves my goals.
