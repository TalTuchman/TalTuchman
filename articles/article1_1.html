The Strategus

A leader of people and operation who acts in a defined Arena. The Strategus is a constant learner who seeks to understand the true nature of reality.

The Strategus is in constant movement in order to learn from being in friction with reality. He possesses high self awareness and thinks in terms of range of constraints and degrees of freedom.

Most importantly, the Strategus strives to bring success & happiness to those he lead.

The strategus is searching for:

  • Purpose
  • Position
  • Understanding
  • Degrees of freedom

The strategus is creating:

  • Action
  • Learning
  • Explanation (graphical presentation of understanding)
